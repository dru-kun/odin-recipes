Eggz.

The perfect dish for anyone to wake up to.

Enjoy a dish so easy anyone can make it. This dish can also be made in a variety of ways and is a staple in breakfast cuisine.

Ingredients

  • eggs
  • salt
  • pepper
  • hotsauce (optional)

Instructions

  1. heat pan on medium to high heat
  2. spread oil or butter over the pan
  3. crack desired ammount of eggz onto the pan
  4. cook the eggz until the egg whitez are no longer transparent
  5. (optionally) cook egg whitez fully but leave a runny yoke
