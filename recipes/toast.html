Toast.

A Grown Adult's Breakfast

Whether you are on the go or enjoying a peaceful morning at home, a slice of toast will always hit the spot.

Ingredients

  • sliced bread
  • toaster
  • butter(optional)

Instructions

  1. plug in toaster
  2. put sliced bread into toaster
  3. set desired toast level and toast bread
  4. put desired condiments on top ie: butter, jelly, jam
